1. PROJECT OVERVIEW
Introduction
LYVO is a trust-based digital commerce platform designed to help customers safely discover and transact with legitimate online businesses.
The platform verifies operators through:
•	Ghana Card registration 
•	Identity verification 
•	Video verification 
and protects customer transactions through an escrow payment system integrated with Moolre APIs. > https://docs.moolre.com/
Moolre's APIs follow REST principles and accept requests and return responses in JSON. All our live APIs are available over HTTPS at https://api.moolre.com, while our sandbox environment is available at https://sandbox.moolre.com.
Here, integration will be using the Laravel api and will be decoupled from other parts of the application so we can be able to add other payment providers and switch easily.
________________________________________
Problem Statement
In Ghana, many online transactions occur through:
•	WhatsApp 
•	Instagram 
•	TikTok 
•	Facebook 
There is currently no trusted system that allows buyers to verify whether a seller is legitimate before making a payment.
This leads to:
•	Fraud 
•	Lost money 
•	Low trust in online commerce 

LYVO is a trust-based digital commerce platform that verifies online businesses through identity verification, Ghana Card registration, and video verification, while enabling secure transactions through an escrow system.

The platform should feel like a premium fintech/trust-tech product similar to Stripe, Paystack, or Flutterwave, with a strong focus on security, verification, and trust.
